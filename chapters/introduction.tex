\begin{abstract}
    The Blockchain, despite its popularity and presence on the internet and in the financial sector, is a relatively new technology and this fact is proved by the large amount of attacks that took place on such networks. This situation raises the need for secure and robust networks that can keep the clientsâ€™ financial assets safe. Fortunately, there are various solutions to this category of problems, like Slither or Manticore, which take different approaches towards finding vulnerabilities. The aim of our paper is to highlight the strenghts of Slither as a static analysis tool and present a solution that integrates it in a streamlined manner and eases the process of scanning blockchain projects for beginner and intermediate developers.
\end{abstract}
